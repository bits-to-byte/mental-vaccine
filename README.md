## Mental Vaccine

### Description 

This panedemic has drastically affected the mental health of individuals.We hereby present Mental Vaccine, a smart android chat bot  which engages and interacts with the user and gives them a personalised experience to luster their day and helping them to overcome mental stress.
As we know too much information during this pandemic can lead to an increase in mental stress so we have implemented geo-specific covid updates that can show you all the relevant information regarding current sitaution in your city avoiding un-necessary updates and information to reduce mental stress.
Similarly we all appreciate a good advice or uplifting quote after a long stressful day, so we have trained an AI algorithm to detect your mood and lusture you with realted quotes and advices. 

### Why Mental vaccine ? 
Just as vaccine is important for physical well-being and protection against corona virus similarly Mental vaccine can cure stessfull minds,englighten mood and help user to cope up with a bad day.

If you get stressed with too much unwanted information regrading current situation around the world then use Mental vaccine special information feature to get updated with only relevant information around you that matters the most,hence reducing unwanted stress.

or if you had a bad day and you are looking for a good advice or motivational thoughts relevant to your mood then use mental vaccine as it uses an AI algorithm to detect user's mood,analyse it and respond accordingly.

### :star: Features 

1. Mood detector to detect your mood and respond accordingly.
2. Voice assistant for easy and hands-free use
3. Location specific important panedemic updates to reduce mental stress.
4. Display Family health statistics
5. Quotes/Inspirational videos manager to organise your most favorite items for your easy access and personalised response.
